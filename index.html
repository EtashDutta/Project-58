Forza Horizon 4 is a 2018 racing video game developed by Playground Games and published by Microsoft Studios.[2] It was released on 2 October 2018 on Xbox One and Microsoft Windows after being announced at Xbox's E3 2018 conference.[3][4] An enhanced version of the game was released on Xbox Series X/S on 10 November 2020.
